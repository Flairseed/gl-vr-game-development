About Guided Learning

Guided Learning is a subject about self-directed learning, where students get choose the area of study.

The subject uses the Plan, Perform, Monitor and Reflect (PPMR) framework, where students will plan and refine their own project prorposal and plan, then they will perform that plan and then finally monitor and reflect on their learning progress.

The project will then be showcased through a curated e-portfolio such as this one. The purpose of this subject is to introduce the process of concepts of self-directed learning and deepend the student's knowledge and skills.

About Virtual Reality Game Development

The area of study of my project is Game Development, specifically in Virtual Reality.

Virtual Reality is a new and growing technology that is used to immerse people into a computer generated world, allowing them to interact with environment in a realistic way. This is maninly done through head-mounted displays, which typically use lens and displays to present the virtual world, and sensors to track the position and movement of the user.

A lot has changed since the release of the prominent Oculus Rift Headset. There have been many improvements in the display and tracking technology in general, however, the pursuit of more immersive worlds has given rise to technologies such as hand tracking, haptics, eye tracking and many more.

Because of the many technologies packed into mordern day head-mounted displays, this technology has many use cases such as productivity, art, education and training. However, game development has always been a big area that is explored by developers.

In this project, I will explore areas such as game design, comfort, 3D modelling, haptics, sound design, optimisation and scripting. I will be using the Unity Game Engine with the OpenXR plugin. I will be using a Meta Quest 3 head-mounted display
